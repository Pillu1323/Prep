// Aggregation framework

Its in core Aggregation framework is just alternative to find method, you could say, you have 
your collection, and Aggregation framework is all about building up pipeline of steps that runs
on the data that is retive from your collection, and then gives you the output in the form you
needed. And these steps are 

collection => {$match} => {$sort} => {$group} => {$project} => output(list of documents)
